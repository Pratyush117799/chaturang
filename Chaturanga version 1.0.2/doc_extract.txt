Undo/Redo: Allowed only after a full round (A→B→C→D ) completes . Talking more detailed about dice roll, there must be 4 dices beside the board for each player. Condition: Enemy team captures one Raja Later, enemy captures one Raja from your team Effect: Pawn promotion may revive a dead Raja, if: Promotion square corresponds to Raja’s original symmetric square That Raja is currently dead Revival replaces pawn; pawn is removed No multiple kings allowed simultaneously. PROMOTION RULE (NARA) Nara promotes only by straight advance Promotion square = symmetric mirror of original major/minor placement Promotion options: Ratha Ashwa Danti Raja- If the raja has been captured once; else if raja is present, the piece will not be able to promote and hence cannot move. No choice outside symmetry .
